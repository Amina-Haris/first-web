HAND EMBROIDERY

Embroidery is the craft of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn. In modern days, embroidery is usually seen on

  1. Hats
  2. Coats
  3. Blankets
  4. Denim
  5. Scarf
  6. Dresses etc

Some of the basic techniques or stitches of the earliest embroidery are

  • Chain stitch
  • Running stitch
  • Satin stitch
  • Button hole or Blanket stitch
